## 9. Acceptance Criteria

### 9.1 Project Completion Criteria

The project will be considered complete when:

1. [ ] All deliverables listed in Section 2.2 are accepted
2. [ ] All acceptance criteria in Section 3.1 are met
3. [ ] All critical and high defects are resolved
4. [ ] UAT is completed and signed off
5. [ ] Production deployment is successful
6. [ ] Training is delivered
7. [ ] Knowledge transfer is complete
8. [ ] Final invoice is paid

### 9.2 Defect Severity Definitions

| Severity | Definition | Resolution Target |
|----------|-----------|------------------|
| Critical | System unusable, data loss, security breach | 24 hours |
| High | Major feature broken, workaround difficult | 3 business days |
| Medium | Feature partially working, workaround available | 1 week |
| Low | Cosmetic issue, no functional impact | Next release |
